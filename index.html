Cara Nixon is a reporter and graduate journalism student currently living in Berkeley, Calif. She graduated in June 2022 with an honors bachelor's degree in political science and applied journalism from Oregon State University and has over four years of print and digital journalism experience. She now attends University of California, Berkeley's Graduate School of Journalism and works with Richmond Confidential and the Investigative Reporting Program's Aging in America project. Prior to coming to California, Nixon worked in both reporter and editor positions at local Corvallis, Ore. newspapers, The Corvallis Advocate and The Daily Barometer.
